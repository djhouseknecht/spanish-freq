Propose

There are many sources that compile the most frequently used words in the Spanish language. Lists like this are helpful for people who are learning Spanish to know which words are the most crucial to learn and understand first. The purpose of this application is to aggregate that data into a clean format for people to quickly look through. The main advantage purpose is to aggregate the Lemma of the most commonly used words (mainly the verbs).

What is a Lemma?

According to Wikipedia.org:

In morphology and lexicography, a lemma (plural lemmas or lemmata) is the canonical form, dictionary form, or citation form of a set of words (headword).

Most specifically in Spanish there are many verb congujations that share the same lemma. For example, está, estoy, & estaba all have the same lemma, estar. This aggregated data can be useful to those learning Spanish to see which verbs and conjugation tenses are most used in Spanish.

Data & Credits

All the data for this project was attained from Wiktionary.org's Spanish frequency lists. They compiled the top 10,000 words from subtitles of Spanish movies. A total of 27.4 million words were compiled (source)

Note: this project does not contain the entire Spanish dictionary. Only the top 10,000 most frequently used words.
